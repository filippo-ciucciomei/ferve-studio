About Ferve

Considered design and styling solutions for Floral, Set, Events, Retail, Campaigns.

FERVE is a fantasy name from the Italian "fervore” (fervour): it embodies the vivacity of Francesca, visual creative behind this multidisciplinary London studio encompassing floral design, set styling, curation and art direction. Francesca has spent over a decade refining her distinct taste as an architect and designer: the outcome is an iridescent yet classy signature touch, rich in timeless references and cutting edge experiments alike. Animated by an endless curiosity, Francesca draws inspiration from vibrant cities at the forefront of culture. She is influenced by characterful interiors, pioneering designers, avant-garde showrooms, emerging artists and masters, daring fashion brands, and any genuine manifestation of talent through bold statements. Blending design knowledge with aesthetic intuition, Ferve turns abstract concepts into natural and spatial narratives that are rooted in seasonality and sustainability, balancing soulfulness with grace.
